  -- 2013  [pill: Assessment] --
  Partnering with CMOs
  We begin working with charter management organizations on assessment-driven
  learning, data-driven instruction, and assessment reporting.

  -- 2014  [pill: First hire] --
  Curriculum takes root
  Ava Millstone joins to lead curriculum, and the work grows from analysis into
  building the slides, guides, and facilitator materials our partners teach from.

  -- 2015  [pill: Team] --
  The team grows
  Alicia Chipman joins, expanding our curriculum-design capacity, and April
  Graham joins, growing our postsecondary ideation and access work.

  -- 2016  [pill: iMentor] --
  Foundations & a first redesign
  We hit our stride in media-rich, asynchronous learning — and deliver our first
  major redesign package for iMentor's multigrade postsecondary program.

  -- 2017  [pill: Milestone] --
  A breakout year
  Creative Director Asha Kelly joins, bringing design and media to our curriculum
  and print media; we sign our first six-figure contracts; and our NMSI
  curriculum work aligns to NGSS.

  -- 2018  [pill: Platforms] --
  Our first app & LMS
  We build our first app and launch our first learning management system with TTK.

  -- 2019  [pill: eLearning] --
  eLearning & STEM take off
  Nadia Zaid joins and we launch our first eLearning project for Early Learning
  Indiana; Cara Johnson joins and builds out our STEM specialization, including
  the Nano experience; and our PLTW work deepens professional-development alignment.

  -- 2020  [pill: COVID pivot] --
  The crisis turn to LMS
  When the world went remote, we helped partners stand up learning management
  systems and move whole programs online, fast — as Erin McIntee joins, bringing
  expert, complex Storyline development.

  -- 2021  [pill: Growth] --
  Media & adult learning
  Michaela Bradley joins and we build out in-house media production, while Morgan
  Taveras brings adult-learning expertise and character-driven design.

  -- 2023  [pill: Growth] --
  Mastering the blend
  A wave of new teammates joins as we hit our stride pairing live facilitation
  with self-paced, media-rich modules.

  -- 2024  [pill: AI / Agentic] --
  Learning gets agentic
  The StriveTogether Continuous Improvement Toolkit ships with scenario-based
  interactions and an AI-powered chatbot — learning that responds.

  -- 2025  [pill: Platforms] --
  Apps, dashboards & custom platforms
  We add app design for data dashboards, custom LMS builds, and agentic feedback
  — software that wraps around the learning.

  -- 2026  [pill: Today] --
  Fifteen years on
  A 21-person studio spanning instructional design, eLearning, media, platforms,
  and strategy — still growing together.
