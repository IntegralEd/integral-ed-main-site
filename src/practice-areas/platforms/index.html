LMS & Platforms

Choosing the right LMS can be a high stakes decision. We support platform evaluation, branded trials, and head-to-head comparisons of costs and features at your scale using your own content.

Good learning also requires the right delivery environment. We help organizations think through the platforms, systems, and implementation choices that support online and blended learning.

This includes LMS selection, digital workflow design, portal development, and practical decisions about how users access and experience learning content.

Typical work includes

  • LMS evaluation and selection
  • Platform pilots and branded trials
  • Digital workflow design
  • Learning portal design
  • Platform implementation planning

How this connects
