Storyline Developer

  • Contract
  • Remote (US)
  • eLearning Development

We're looking for an Articulate Storyline developer who can turn instructional designs and storyboards into polished, accessible, interactive eLearning. You'd build scenario-based modules, custom interactions, and SCORM-ready courses for educators, students, job seekers, and adult learners — work that has to function cleanly for real users, not just look good in a demo.

What you'd do

  • Build interactive modules in Articulate Storyline (and Rise) from storyboards and design specs
  • Develop custom interactions, scenarios, and assessments
  • Produce SCORM/xAPI-conformant packages that deploy reliably across LMSs
  • Collaborate with instructional designers, media producers, and project leads
  • QA across devices and browsers, and meet accessibility standards

What we look for

  • A strong portfolio of Storyline work — custom interactions, not just templates
  • Comfort with SCORM/LMS publishing and basic troubleshooting
  • Care for clarity, accessibility, and the learner experience
  • Reliable, communicative, and good at managing your time on a remote team
  • Bonus: HTML/CSS/JS, Rise, media editing, or instructional design experience
